Module 9: Worker Knowledge Transfer System

Problem Statement

In Vishalanagari's bustling factories and service hubs, invaluable tacit knowledge — quick fixes, machine-specific tips, and local heuristics — evaporates during shift handovers. Incoming workers waste time rediscovering solutions, errors reappear, and overall throughput suffers. We need a gentle, intelligent keeper of workplace wisdom that captures on-the-floor insights in real-time, organizes them for instant lookup, and hands the right guidance to the next person on duty. The system should prioritize critical notes, surface recent shift highlights, and make finding relevant knowledge as natural as typing a single keyword. The result: fewer repeated mistakes, faster problem resolution, and a workforce that learns as it works.

Algorithms Used

Trie: Stores worker notes and keywords efficiently, allowing instant retrieval even from partial input.

Skip List: Maintains chronological shift updates with fast insertion and lookup for recent insights.

Red-Black Tree: Organizes insights by priority for predictable, fast access.

2-3 Tree: Groups related knowledge categories in a balanced tree, ensuring uniform search performance.

Algorithm Efficiency

Trie: Insert & search operations scale with input length (O(m)), enabling fast keyword lookups.

Skip List: Insert, delete, and search operations are expected O(log n), efficiently handling chronological logs.

Red-Black Tree: Insert, delete, and search operations are guaranteed O(log n), ensuring prioritized insights are quickly accessible.

2-3 Tree: Balanced tree ensures O(log n) search, insert, and delete, making grouped knowledge retrieval predictable.

How This Aligns with SDGs

SDG 8 — Decent Work & Economic Growth: Preserves practical skills and reduces downtime, enhancing worker efficiency and smooth operations.

SDG 9 — Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure: Builds robust digital infrastructure for knowledge continuity and resilient operations.

SDG 4 — Quality Education: Encourages on-the-job learning by surfacing proven solutions and tacit expertise to all workers.
