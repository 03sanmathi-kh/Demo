Our Vision

Designing Tomorrow, One Algorithm at a Time

Vishalanagari, located in Maharashtra’s Vidarbha region between Amravati and Akola, is a next-generation smart city built on the fertile lands of the Deccan Plateau. The Purna River supports rich agriculture, making the region India’s premier cotton-growing and textile hub with major export networks. Designed for 28 million people with a density of 18,000/km², the city operates on an annual budget of ₹18,000 crore and is recognized nationwide as India’s capital of civic innovation. Within city limits, 100% electric vehicles ensure zero-emission transport, while smart algorithms manage traffic, agriculture, industries, commercial hubs, governance systems, and sustainability operations — forming a balanced and efficient urban ecosystem.
Strong roots, smart shoots.
